Do Not Sleep during Magrhib Time (Before and After Sunset)

Description:
In some parts of Indonesia, it is believed that sleeping during Maghrib (the time just before or after sunset) can bring bad luck, attract spirits, or cause misfortune. People believe it's better to stay awake and avoid sleeping at this time.

Irrational Reason:
During Maghrib, evil spirits, including Satan, are said to be more active or roaming around. People who sleep at this time are believed to be vulnerable to these spirits, who might "sit" on them or cause harm.

Rational Reason:
One rational explanation behind the superstition is that sleeping during Maghrib may disrupt with the Maghrib prayer, which is considered a vital spiritual practice. When people sleep during this time, they might miss the prayer or perform it late.
